Calle 13 Tribute page!

Bio

Calle 13, English 13th Street, Puerto Rican popular music duo known for intelligent, poetic, and sharply pointed social and political commentary.

All delivered through a distinctive blend of hip-hop with a broad range of Latin American music styles.
